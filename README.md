# Rails & Risk

A practical fintech operations and compliance playbook focused on onboarding, payment workflows, risk operations, escalation handling, and transaction lifecycle management.

This repository is designed to document real-world operational systems that support modern payments infrastructure, compliance operations, merchant onboarding, exception handling, and cross-functional fintech workflows.

Topics include:
- KYC and onboarding workflows
- Payment operations
- Escalation management
- Transaction monitoring concepts
- Risk review processes
- Operational workflow design
- Cross-functional coordination systems
- Payments lifecycle operations

Built from hands-on experience across fintech, payments, compliance operations, and emerging financial technology environments.
